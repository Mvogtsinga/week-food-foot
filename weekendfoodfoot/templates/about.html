Who are we?

Weekend Food Foot is an ambitious project in theme-based catering.Football is one of the favorite sports in England, our big challenge is to combine culinary arts and football every weekend. the idea came from Aubin KOENIGSSOHN, a former professional footballer and passionate about cooking. We have a team of chefs to take you on a journey through the flavors of the world.Every detail from top to bottom has been carefully considered. Take a time and visit our restaurant in Birmingham.
